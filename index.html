The Unbelievable Smell of Tom

Tom, a well-known figure in his community, has recently been the subject of widespread rumours, with some theories on his recent stench being deemed "wild" by avid reader, Poppy. Tommy Queue (born 10th April) is an man who recently developed a rather alarming stench. He was observed last Wednesday going for a walk to see his well known friend Jim, he was later seen stumbling out of the house looking rather intoxicated. A reporter from our paper was sent to examine the scene and they found something rather surprising... A pile of clothes, a bunch of fur (potentially dog) and a small puddle of blood! One of the most common theories about this new and abhorrent stench is that is related to the recently "disease" outbreak in the local area. We contacted a local expert to try and add substance to the claims of a lycanthropy outbreak in the Lower Westside of Cambridge city.
